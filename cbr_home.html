ABOUT

Welcome to Chicago Bear Rescue, an organization that is concerned with bear wellness and safety. We are devoted to making bears thrive in all environments, including this bustling city. We provide services for bears and the humans that want to be associated with them. Our services include bear adoptions, supporting bear research and educating the community. We hope that you learn something from your visit to our website and consider adopting one of our bears to help their on their journey to maximum plumpness.
